Fashion Is Not
What You Wear.
It Is How You
Exist.

Founded in 2008 by creative director Élise Varon, FashionWorld began as a small atelier in the Marais district of Paris. What started as a rejection of seasonal disposability became a manifesto for permanence — clothing made to outlast trends, designed for the body that inhabits them.

Today, FashionWorld presents four collections annually across Paris, Milan, Tokyo, and New York. Every piece is designed with the same conviction: that fashion, at its finest, is indistinguishable from art.
